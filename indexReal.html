About Us


Welcome to our flower farm — a small, heartfelt venture dedicated to growing beautiful, chemical-free cut flowers. We specialize in seasonal subscription bouquets, lovingly arranged and delivered fresh from our fields to your home. Every bloom is a tribute, created in memory of our baby Andrew, whose spirit inspires us to share beauty, kindness, and life with others — one bouquet at a time.

Delivery Information

We offer local delivery with care and attention to detail. Whether it's for a celebration, remembrance, or a simple act of kindness, your flowers will arrive beautifully arranged and thoughtfully presented.

Click & collect is available from our flower farm in Killygordon. We'll send full instructions after your order is placed.

Gift Vouchers

Looking for a thoughtful gift? We offer beautifully presented gift vouchers that can be used toward any of our floral arrangements or services. Perfect for any occasion and available in a range of values.

We offer next day postal delivery on voucher orders placed before 3pm. We're also happy to personalise and send directly on your behalf.
